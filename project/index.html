NEEDED - Nordic SEas sEDimentary anciEnt DNA: Sedimentary ancient DNA - a new proxy to investigate the impact of environmental change on past and present biodiversity in Nordic Seas.

We will analyze sediment DNA in the six well-dated cores collected at Svalbard, Greenland and Jan Mayen shelves. By using multiple genetic markers, we will follow changes in composition of different groups of organisms, from microalgae to single-cell protozoans and animals. We will integrate these historical biodiversity data with the information about environmental change provided by classical palaeoceanographic proxies. The results of our study will help understanding how the marine organisms respond to climate change in the past and whether this response is similar to what we observe today. Project no. 2019/34/H/ST10/00682 . [Online Thesis]
