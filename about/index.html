About Me

Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Environmental DNA, Department of Paleoceanography, Institute of Oceanology Polish Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on environmental DNA (eDNA), microbial genomics, marine microbial ecology, biodiversity assessment, and bioinformatics. I am particularly interested in developing innovative molecular and computational approaches for understanding marine biodiversity and ecosystem functioning.

Research Interests

  • Environmental DNA (eDNA)
  • Marine Microbial Ecology
  • Marine Biodiversity
  • Metabarcoding
  • Microbial Genomics
  • Bioinformatics
  • Artificial Intelligence for Ecology
  • Long-read Sequencing
  • Nanopore Sequencing

Current Research

My current projects include global analyses of benthic foraminiferal diversity, fish farming impact assessment using eDNA metabarcoding, development of bioinformatic pipelines, and machine learning applications for marine ecology.
