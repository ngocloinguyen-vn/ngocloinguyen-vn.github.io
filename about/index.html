About Me

Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Environmental DNA, Department of Paleoceanography, Institute of Oceanology Polish Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on environmental DNA (eDNA), microbial genomics, marine biodiversity, metabarcoding, and bioinformatics. I develop molecular and computational approaches for biodiversity assessment and environmental monitoring in marine ecosystems.

Research Interests

  • Environmental DNA (eDNA)
  • Marine Microbial Ecology
  • Marine Biodiversity
  • Metabarcoding
  • Oxford Nanopore Sequencing
  • Microbial Genomics
  • Artificial Intelligence in Ecology
  • R Programming
  • Bioinformatics

Current Projects

Global Foraminiferal Biogeography

Fish Farm Monitoring

Assessing aquaculture impacts using benthic eDNA.

ForamSign

Developing an open-source R package for benthic foraminiferal sequence identification.
