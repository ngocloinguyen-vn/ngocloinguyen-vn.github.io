Tutorials

Since I’m not an expert, I’ve created this note as a simple, practical place to keep track of the R code I’ve used to generate visualizations and perform statistical analyses on microbial ecology datasets. While my focus here is on microbial amplicon, the methods and workflows I share can be easily adapted to any dataset organized by samples as ASV/OTU or species abundances. Since there are many ways to achieve similar results in R, I encourage you to run and modify the code to best suit your own data and research questions. Please feel free to reach out with feedback, issues, comments, or suggestions, as well as if you’re interested in discussing potential collaborations or opportunities where my expertise might be beneficial.
