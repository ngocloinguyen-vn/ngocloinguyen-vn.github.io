Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Environmental DNA, Department of Paleoceanography, Institute of Oceanology Polish Academy of Sciences. My research integrates environmental DNA (eDNA), marine microbial ecology, genomics, and bioinformatics to investigate marine biodiversity and ecosystem functioning.

Research Areas

Environmental DNA

Marine biodiversity assessment using metabarcoding and long-read sequencing.

Bioinformatics

Developing computational tools and R packages for metabarcoding analyses.

Marine Ecology

Studying benthic biodiversity and microbial communities across global oceans.
