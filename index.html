Welcome

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Environmental DNA, Department of Paleoceanography, Institute of Oceanology Polish Academy of Sciences. My research combines environmental DNA (eDNA), marine microbial ecology, metabarcoding, genomics, and bioinformatics to investigate marine biodiversity, ecosystem functioning, and environmental monitoring.

Research Interests

Environmental DNA

Using metabarcoding and sequencing technologies for biodiversity monitoring.

Marine Ecology

Investigating marine microbial and benthic communities across environmental gradients.

Bioinformatics

Developing computational tools for metabarcoding analysis and ecological interpretation.
